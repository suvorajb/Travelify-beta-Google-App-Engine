TraveliNearby ... Quickly find nearby restaurants, bar, cafe, spa, shopping centers etc. with your mobile device in your neighborhood

1Click TraveliNearby. It will ask for your location. Select Allow and it will show you all nearby places... just play around for whatever you are looking for...

2Once you find a place and if you want to book-mark it for future access click Clip It and it will be available in your clip list section

3If clipping a place is successful you'll get a pop-up of successful message

TraveliCity ... Going to a new place or city? Find all the popular and top rated resraurants to dine or lunch, cafe, breakfast places, hotels...

1Click TraveliCity and then either select from the popular city list or search and browse...

2Once you find a place and if you want to book-mark it for future access click Clip It and it will be available in your clip list section

3If clipping a place is successful you'll get a pop-up of successful message

TraveliBoards ... Create beautiful place boards with the places you love and help others discover new places...completely crowd sourced

1Click TraveliBoards and it will show you beautiful place boards created by others. Click view and discover new places.

2If you want to share the place board in your Google+ circle click Share in G+ Circle.

3Once the board loads you will view the beautiful board and submit comments

4 To create a board either click Create a Board from the menu or click the button
